The Mutual Agreement Procedures (MAP) provides for dispute resolution through bilateral

Key differences between the OECD Model Convention (Article 25) and UN Model Convention

Article 25B(5) of the UN Model provides that an arbitration may be initiated if the competent
authorities are unable to reach an agreement on a case within three years from the

arbitration decision if they agree to do so within six months after the decision has been
