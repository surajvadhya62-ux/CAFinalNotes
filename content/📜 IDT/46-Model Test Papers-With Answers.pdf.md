The facts of the given case are similar to a Supreme Court judgment3 wherein the Court held that unless an assessment order has been reviewed and/or modified in an appeal, that assessment order stands, and the duty is payable only as per that assessment order.  
 A refund claim is not an appeal proceeding. 
Further, the officer considering the refund claim, cannot not review the assessment order.  
Thus, refund claims based on challenge to an order of assessment are liable to be rejected.
