## File Naming Convention
Notes follow this pattern: `{number}-{title}.md` or `{number}-{title}.pdf.md`
- The number prefix indicates ordering within the NotebookLM export
- `.pdf.md` suffix means the note was extracted from a PDF source
- Plain `.md` files are typically from YouTube video transcripts or user-created notes
